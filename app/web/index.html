The Evolution of Knowledge Tools

1945

Memex

Vannevar Bush imagines a personal device for storing and linking books, records and communications.

1980s

Hypertext

Ted Nelson and others popularize linked documents — the seed of every wiki and web browser today.

1995

WikiWikiWeb

Ward Cunningham launches the first wiki, letting anyone edit interconnected pages collaboratively.

2010s

Personal Knowledge Graphs

Tools like Roam, Obsidian, and Logseq make backlinks and graph views mainstream for individuals.

2020s

Local-first AI Wikis

Your notes, PDFs, and folders become structured knowledge bases — without leaving your machine.
